Money Flow

Money Flow​

How does earning distribution work for the different entities (games, guild founders, seat owners, and fraction onwers)? Below is a more detailed breakdown on how money flows in the ecosystem. Please note that the fee structure here is not fixed and can be adjusted due to commercial agreements.

Stage 1: Earning Distribution from Fraction Trades​

Assuming a fraction gets sold for $100, a total of 10% fee will apply - 5% distributed to ShardsTech treasury / Game partner and 5% to the guild. This means ShardsTech / Game partner and guild earns $5 each from this transaction.

Stage 2: Earning Distribution among Guild Entities at the end of the season​

Now, assuming the guild only earned $5 throughout the season, how will that be distributed among the entities? Earnings will be allocated according to the earning splits set by the founder upon guild creation. In this scenario, guild founder gets 20%, fraction owners get 20%, and seat owners get 60%. This means each entity gets $1, $1, $3, respectively.

Stage 3: Earning Distribution among Fraction Holders at the end of the season​

Total earnings for the fraction holders are evenly split among all holders. In this scenario, the $1 share gets divided among the 3 fraction holders, giving them $0.33 each.

Stage 4: Earning Distribution among Seat Owners at the end of the season​

Total earnings for the players or seat owners are split in proportion to their guild contributions. In this scenario, if P1 did 50% of the work for their guild, then P1 gets 50% of the total allocation for the seat owner earnings, giving the player $1.50 out of the total pot of $3.
