Flywheel

Shards was designed to amplify the synergies between GameFi and SocialFi thereby kick-starting a more sustainable value flywheel. The Shards Flywheel forms the beating heart of the Shards economy whereby all participants are aligned towards creating great shared gaming experiences.

The Shards Flywheel consists of the following high-level concepts:

  • More Social Awareness: The success of the Team and the popularity of their Players drives increased awareness of the game and the Shards Platform.

  • Generate Increased Demand: Team popularity and potential will drive organic demand for the Team’s Fractions as Non-Players will want to be associated with the team and maybe even receive additional Team-specific perks.

  • Increased Fraction Ownership: With every additional Fraction purchased, the price per Fraction increases (per bonding curve) resulting in more value being shared for every transaction.

  • Increased Game Value: With revenue from Fraction swaps being shared with all Shards participants, this will drive increased value capture for the game.

  • More Revenue Share: The increase in game value supports other core game functions as well as the in-game economy resulting in higher transaction volumes and greater revenue share.

  • Team Advancement: The teams that are able to expand their support base will receive even more indirect revenue via the swapping of Fractions thereby enabling them to grow.
